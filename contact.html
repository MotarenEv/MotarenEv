Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our electric scooters

What is the warranty period for Motarenev scooters?

All Motarenev scooters come with a comprehensive 5-year warranty covering the battery, motor, and electronics. We also provide 2 years of free servicing and 24/7 roadside assistance.

How long does it take to charge the battery?

Charging time varies by model: Motarenev Lite takes 2.5 hours, Standard takes 3 hours, and Pro takes 4 hours for a full charge. All models support fast charging.

What is the range of your electric scooters?

Our scooters offer excellent range: Motarenev Lite (40km), Standard (60km), and Pro (80km) on a single charge. Range may vary based on riding conditions and load.

Absolutely! Our scooters are designed with powerful motors and advanced torque management systems that perform excellently on inclines up to 15 degrees.
